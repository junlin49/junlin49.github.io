CMake编译OpenCV-contrib

  • 将opencv_contrib-4.3.0放置在F:\DownLoad\opencv目录下，在CMake中找到OPENCV_EXTRA_MODULES_PATH，填入F:\DownLoad\opencv\opencv_contrib-4.3.0\modules路径，并勾选上方OPENCV_ENABLE_NONFREE；
